Batman - The History

Batman is a legendary superhero created by artist Bob Kane and writer Bill Finger, first appearing in Detective Comics #27 in 1939. As Bruce Wayne, he is a wealthy businessman who, after witnessing the tragic murder of his parents as a child, swears to fight crime and injustice. Operating out of Gotham City, Batman uses his intellect, detective skills, martial arts expertise, and advanced technology to combat villains, often without relying on superhuman abilities. His iconic symbol, the bat, represents fear and justice. Known for his brooding persona, Batman remains one of the most influential and enduring characters in comic book history.
